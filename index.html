Privacy Policy

App: BMTC Bus Tracker (Unofficial)
Developer: Kanaksan
Package: com.kanaksan.bmtc
Effective date: June 2026

1. Overview

BMTC Bus Tracker is an unofficial Android application that displays real-time bus tracking information for the Bengaluru Metropolitan Transport Corporation (BMTC) network. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what is never collected or shared.

2. Data we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information.
  • We do not collect your device's GPS location.
  • We do not create user accounts or require any sign-in.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or sell data to third parties.
  • We do not track your usage behaviour or analytics.

3. Data stored on your device

All data the app stores remains exclusively on your device:

  • Route & stop data — synced once weekly from the BMTC public API and cached in a local Room database for offline use.
  • Favourites — buses and routes you save are stored locally in Room.
  • App preferences — settings such as refresh interval and notification preferences are stored in Android SharedPreferences.
  • Issue reports — any feedback you submit via "Report an Issue" is stored locally only; it is not transmitted to any external server in this version.

4. Network communication

The app communicates exclusively with the BMTC public API (bmtcmobileapi.karnataka.gov.in) to fetch live bus positions, route details, and stop data. No user-identifying information is sent in these requests — only bus numbers and route IDs that you explicitly search for.

The app also communicates with Google's Maps SDK servers to render map tiles. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Permissions

  • INTERNET — required to fetch live bus data from the BMTC API.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — used to check connectivity before making API calls.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — used to fire stop-approach alarms when you set a stop alert. You can deny this permission; the app will work normally without notifications.
  • VIBRATE — used alongside stop-approach notifications.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — declared for potential future use (e.g., "find buses near me"). The app does not currently read your device location in this version.

6. Third-party services

  • Google Maps SDK — Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services — Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects crash reports (device model, OS version, stack trace) to help fix bugs. No personal data is included. Firebase Privacy Policy

No advertising or behavioural tracking SDKs are included.

7. Children's privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.
